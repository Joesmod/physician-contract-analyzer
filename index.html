Don't Sign a Physician Contract You Don't Fully Understand.

Your employment contract is the most consequential financial document of your career. Most physicians sign it without knowing what's buried inside.

Here's What's at Stake

  • Tail coverage traps — Claims-made malpractice insurance could leave you owing $50,000–$200,000+ when you leave.
  • Non-compete clauses — A 25-mile, 2-year non-compete could force you to uproot your family or sit idle.
  • Termination without cause — Your employer can end with 90 days' notice. Can you? What happens to your tail, your bonus?
  • RVU thresholds & productivity traps — Benchmarks that are unrealistic or change at employer's discretion.
  • Liquidated damages — Penalties of $100K+ if you leave early. Even if they fire you.
